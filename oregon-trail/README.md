# The Oregon Trail

A 2,040 mile journey from Independence, Missouri to Oregon's Willamette Valley — 1848.

[Play Now](https://aria-nexus.github.io/ARIA/oregon-trail/)

## How to Play
- Choose your profession (Banker, Carpenter, or Farmer)
- Name your 5 party members
- Buy supplies and manage resources along the trail
- Hunt for food, cross rivers, and survive random events
- Keep your party alive to reach Oregon
